Expert Guide: Duplicating Cpl. Goblins for Mutation Fusion

🏠

Field Farming & Respawns

Since Cpl. Goblins are common field enemies, your primary source is high-volume clearing.

  • Daily Resets: Field mobs respawn once every 24 hours. Map out their locations in the early regions.
  • Full Clear: Do not just hunt the bosses. Small goblin clusters are more likely to drop duplicates in bulk.
  • Nyanners Mastery: Ensure your companion level is maxed to increase the capture rate of goblin souls.

Dimensional Rift Optimization

Rifts offer a higher concentration of material drops but require more focus.

  • The Exit Strategy: If the rift Boss isn't the specific variant you need, exit before completion to save high-tier resources (check current patch notes for stamina refunds).
  • Targeted Nodes: Only enter nodes marked with "Monsterling Drop" icons.

The Mutation Process

Once you have gathered a stockpile of duplicates, follow these steps to trigger the mutation:

  1. Navigate to Monsterling > Combine.
  2. Select the Fusion Tab (Third Icon).
  3. Insert your Cpl. Goblins as the primary material.
  4. Check the Inheritance box if you have a duplicate with high base stats.
Mutation Tip

Mutation chance is low. Using duplicates with Matching Traits can sometimes stabilize the outcome.
